## 1. Data Collection and Usage

### 1.1 Account Registration & Authentication

#### 1.1.1 Email Registration
When you register or sign in with email, we collect:
- **Email address**: For account registration, authentication, and account recovery
- **Encrypted password**: Stored using bcrypt hashing (salt rounds: 10). We cannot access your plain-text password.

#### 1.1.2 Google Sign-In (OAuth)
When you sign in with Google, we collect through Google's OAuth service:
- **Google unique ID**: Your unique identifier within our application
- **Email address**: Associated with your Google account
- **Display name**: Your Google profile name
- **Profile picture**: Your Google avatar (optional)

> **Legal Basis**: We process this data based on contract performance (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)) - providing the service you requested.

### 1.2 AI Generation Services & Data Handling

#### 1.2.1 AI Generation
Your AI generation requests are processed as follows:
- Input content is sent to third-party AI API providers (e.g., Alibaba Qwen, Mistral AI, Replicate) for processing
- **We do not store your generation records on our servers for training purposes**
- Generation history is saved to your account only when logged in, for viewing history
- Guest users' generation records are NOT saved

#### 1.2.2 Document Generation
- Supports generating Word, Excel, PDF and other format documents
- Generated documents are stored in cloud storage services (Tencent CloudBase / Supabase)
- **Documents are used solely for your download and history viewing, not for other commercial purposes**

#### 1.2.3 Image Generation
- Uses Replicate API for image generation
- Generated images are stored in cloud storage services
- **Images are used solely for your download and history viewing**

#### 1.2.4 Audio Generation
- Uses third-party AI services for audio generation
- Generated audio is stored in cloud storage services
- **Audio is used solely for your download and history viewing**

#### 1.2.5 Video Generation
- Uses Replicate API for video generation
- Generated videos are stored in cloud storage services
- **Videos are used solely for your download and history viewing**

**Legal Basis**: Your use of generation services constitutes explicit consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)).
